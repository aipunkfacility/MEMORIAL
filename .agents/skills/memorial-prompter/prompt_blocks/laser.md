# Laser Engraving Block

1. **Format & Lighting:** Output strictly in Black and White. Use Soft Studio Lighting (Frontal). The face must be well-lit and bright, with smooth transitions, avoiding pitch-black shadows on the skin.

2. **Face & Skin:** Make the skin smooth, yet retain natural, subtle skin nuances (refined micro-pores) to avoid a waxy/plastic look. Remove "dirty" noise. The face should look like a high-quality airbrushed portrait. Do NOT add aging effects or harsh micro-texture.
